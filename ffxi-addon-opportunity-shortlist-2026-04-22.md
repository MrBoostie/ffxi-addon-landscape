## Top 5 build opportunities

1. **OnboardingPilot** (general_qol)
   - What: Guided setup wizard for first-run + sanity checks (addons, commands, keybinds, paths).
   - Why now: High pain, low complexity, underrepresented as dedicated addon.

2. **AddonHealth** (diagnostics)
   - What: Unified dashboard: addon loaded state, errors, packet lag hints, command conflicts.
   - Why now: Ecosystem has many isolated tools, almost no single health pane.

3. **SafeAuto** (combat_automation)
   - What: Policy layer for automation: safe-zones, cooldown caps, panic key, action audit.
   - Why now: Automation exists but guardrails/visibility are inconsistent.

4. **PartyConductor** (combat_automation)
   - What: Party coordination board: SC windows, role reminders, buff ownership.
   - Why now: Assist exists but cross-role planning UX is sparse.

5. **ProfileSync** (general_qol)
   - What: Portable profile exporter/importer across chars with conflict checker.
   - Why now: Config fragmentation across addons is a common pain.
